Privacy Policy

At Syncshift, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our employee time tracking application for Shopify stores.

Quick Summary: We only collect essential time tracking data, never share it with third parties, and use industry-standard security. Your data is isolated to your store and automatically deleted if you uninstall the app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Store Information

When you install Syncshift, we collect basic information about your Shopify store:

  • Store Domain: Your store's myshopify.com URL for app authentication
  • Store ID: Unique identifier to isolate your data
  • App Installation Data: When you installed and last used the app
  • Shopify Plan: Basic plan information for compatibility

1.2 Employee Time Tracking Data

The core functionality of our app involves collecting time tracking information:

Data Type What We Collect Why We Need It
Staff Information Employee names, roles, and ID numbers you create To organize time entries and generate reports
Clock Events Clock in/out, break, and lunch timestamps To calculate work hours and track attendance
Time Entries Date, time, duration, and action type To provide accurate time tracking and payroll data
Device Information POS device ID where entries were created For troubleshooting and data integrity

1.3 Usage Analytics

We collect minimal, anonymized usage data to improve our app:

  • Feature usage frequency (which features are used most)
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Performance metrics (app load times, response times)
  • General usage patterns (without personal identifiers)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary App Functions

We use your data exclusively for providing time tracking services:

  • Time Calculation: Computing work hours, breaks, and overtime
  • Report Generation: Creating daily, weekly, and monthly reports
  • Data Export: Providing CSV/Excel exports for payroll
  • Staff Management: Organizing employees and their time records

2.2 App Improvement

We may use anonymized, aggregated data to:

  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Develop new features based on common usage patterns
  • Ensure compatibility with Shopify updates
  • Optimize user experience and interface design

2.3 Support and Communication

When you contact support, we may use your information to:

  • Respond to your questions and troubleshoot issues
  • Provide technical assistance and guidance
  • Send important app updates or security notices

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Sharing

Important: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or business data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your time tracking data stays private and secure.

We only share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our app (Firebase, hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Security Threats: To investigate fraud or security breaches
  • Business Transfer: If our company is acquired (with 30 days notice)

4.2 Shopify Integration

Our app integrates with Shopify's platform:

  • We use Shopify's OAuth system for secure authentication
  • We access only the minimum required permissions (product write access for POS)
  • Shopify may have access to basic app usage statistics
  • All data exchange follows Shopify's security standards

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View Data: Access all your time tracking data through the admin dashboard
  • Export Data: Download your data in CSV format anytime
  • Edit Data: Modify or correct time entries as needed
  • Delete Data: Remove specific entries or uninstall the app to delete everything

5.2 Data Portability

If you decide to switch time tracking solutions:

  • Export all your historical data before uninstalling
  • Data is provided in standard CSV format
  • Includes all time entries, staff information, and calculated hours
  • Compatible with most payroll and HR systems

5.3 Geographic Rights
