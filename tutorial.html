Using Syncshift on POS
For Employees
For Managers
1
Clock In at Start of Shift

Simple process to start tracking work time:

  • Open Shopify POS on your device
  • Tap the "POS Time Tracking Extension" tile
  • Select your name from the dropdown
  • Choose "Clock In" action
  • Tap "Submit" - you'll see a success message
üì∏ Screenshot: POS time tracking modal with Clock In selected
‚úÖ Success! Your shift has started. The system will track your time until you clock out.
2
Track Breaks and Lunch

Record breaks to ensure accurate time tracking:

  • Going on break: Select "Break" and submit
  • Lunch time: Select "Lunch" and submit
  • Back from break: Select "Clock In" to resume work
üí° Remember: Always clock back in after breaks so your work hours are calculated correctly.
3
Clock Out at End of Shift

Complete your shift tracking:

  • Open the time tracking extension
  • Select your name
  • Choose "Clock Out"
  • Submit to end your shift
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Don't forget to clock out! Missing clock-out entries will show as 0 hours worked.
4
What If You Forget?

No problem! Managers can fix missing entries:

  • Tell your manager about the missed clock in/out
  • They can add the entry from the admin dashboard
  • Or edit existing entries to correct the time

Reports & Analytics
1
Understanding Hour Calculations

How Syncshift calculates work hours:

  • Work Time: Clock Out time minus Clock In time
  • Break Time: Tracked separately, not deducted from work time
  • Lunch Time: Tracked separately for compliance
  • Net Hours: Total productive work time
üìù Example: Clock in 9:00 AM, lunch at 12:00 PM, back at 1:00 PM, clock out 5:00 PM = 7 hours work + 1 hour lunch.

Troubleshooting & Support

‚ùå POS tile not showing up

The time tracking tile isn't visible on the POS home screen.

Solutions:
  • Restart the Shopify POS app completely
  • Check that the app is installed in Shopify admin
  • Ensure POS device has internet connection
  • Update POS app to the latest version
  • Wait 2-3 minutes after installation for tile to appear

‚ùå Time entries not appearing in dashboard

Entries made on POS aren't showing up in the admin dashboard.

Solutions:
  • Refresh the dashboard page
  • Check date range filter includes entry dates
  • Verify staff filter isn't excluding the entries
  • Ensure POS had internet when entry was made
  • Wait 1-2 minutes for data to sync

‚ùå Hours calculation seems wrong

The calculated work hours don't match expected values.

Common Causes:
  • Missing clock out: Shows as 0 hours (add missing entry)
  • Clock out before clock in: Invalid sequence
  • Multiple clock ins: Without clock out between them
  • Time zone issues: In manual entries
  • Break periods: Check if breaks are properly recorded

‚ùå Can't add or edit staff members

Getting errors when trying to manage employee information.

Solutions:
  • Check that you have admin access to the Shopify store
  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Ensure staff name isn't duplicate
  • Check for special characters in names
